Caesar Cipher: Encrypt & Decrypt

Ever wanted to send your friends messages that no one else can read? Try out this program and keep that shift value between you and your friends!

HOW TO USE: User selects either encryption or decryption. The user will then input the appropriate sentence to decrypt or encrypt and will then be asked the shift amount. The correct encryption/decryption sentence will be presented. The software loops until the user wants to exist.
